Meet the team

James

James has been a coach for 3 years. James was the 2009 Eurobasket winner. Coaching the Under 20s & 30s.


He's brought the two teams to the national final in 2020, taking home 1st and 2nd place.


Anna

Anna is recent to the team. Coaching the Childrens and Beginners team.



Anna competed in the 2016 Olympics and was hoping for 2020, but the Olympics got pushed back to 2021.


Harry

Harry has been with the team for 21 years. Coaching Under 40's, 50's & 60's.



Harry and his team competed in the Euroleague championships and won in 2019 & 2020.


Linda

Linda joined the team 6 years ago after retiring from years of competing professionally. Coaching the Secondary School Classes.


Linda is a qualified P.E. teacher, graduating from DCU in 2013.


Kevin

Kevin joined the team 6 months ago. Kevin helps Harry with coaching the Under 40's, 50's & 60's. He also helps sets up the Game Days.


Kevin is currently studying Sports Science in UCD.
