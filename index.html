About Us

Griffith Leisure Club was opened in 1979 and the aim of the club is to provide sporting amenities for the local area. Giffith Leisure Club is situated in Griffith Park, Blackrock, Dublin 4, opposite the Tesco.


Our facilities include a 25 metre swimming pool (swimming, water aerobics, etc.) and large indoor and outdoor basketball courts.


Our Swimming and Basketball teams are the most sucessful in Dublin and ranking 5th over all for the country.


We pride outselves on being the most inclusive Leisure Centre in Ireland, with multiple classes and teams to suit everyone.


Swimming coaches include Olympians and All Ireland winners.


Basketball coaches include EuroLeague Award winners and EuroBasket 2009 winner.
