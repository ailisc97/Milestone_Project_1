About Us


Griffith Leisure Club was opened in 1979 and the aim of the club is to provide sporting amenities for the local area. Giffith Leisure Club is situated in Griffith Park, Blackrock, Dublin 4, opposite the Tesco. Our facilities include a 25 metre swimming pool (swimming, water aerobics, etc.) and large basketball hall.. BR>

Our Swimming and Basketball teams are the most sucessful in Dublin and ranking 5th over all for the country.


Coaches in include Olympians and All Ireland Winners, click our Swimming and Basketball pages for more info.
