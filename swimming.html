Meet the team

Katie

Katie has been with the team for 5 years. Coaching the Childrens team and organising the weekly 100 Meter and 500 Meter races.


In 2016 Katie won Sliver (100 Meter Freestyle) and Bronze (200 Meter Freestyle) in the Olympics.


Kayla

Kayla is recent to the team, after graduating from UCD in Sports Science. Coaching the Childrens and Beginners team.


Kayla swims in the sea every morning and has multiple medals in 100 meter championships.


Brian

Brian has been with the team for 2 years. Teaching Water Aerobics and Water Ballet.



Competing in the National Water Ballet for 10 years and comming top 3 every year.


Jess

Jess joined the team 6 years ago after retiring from years of competing professionally. Coaching the Imtermediate and Advanced teams.


Having competed in the 2008 and 2012 Olympics and a grand total of 4 medals.


David

David has been a coach for 20 years. Coaching the Imtermediate team and organising the monthly Volleyball.



David competes in the Bosphorus Cross-Continental Swim, Turkey, every year.


Adam

Adam has been with the team for 8 years now. Coaching the Advanced team and organising the weekly 100 meter and 500 meter races.


Adam is the National 500 Meter champion, 4 years running.
